Students Discounts

Who is eligible?

To qualify for our student discount, you must be:

  • Enrolled in a full-time or part-time program at a recognized university or college.
  • Able to present a valid student ID card at the time of service.

What discounts are available?

Eligible students receive the following discounts:

  • **20% off** labor charges for all repair services.
  • **5% off** genuine replacement parts (excluding accessories).
  • **Free** diagnostic fee (up to $20 value).

How to claim your discount

  1. Bring your device and valid student ID to our shop.
  2. Mention the student discount to our technician upon arrival.
  3. The discount will be applied to your final bill.

Additional notes:

  • Discounts cannot be combined with other promotions or offers.
  • Discounts are subject to change without notice.
  • For any questions, please contact us at +971-55-2904932 or help@justrepair.net.
